About

As Co-founder and CTO of Subnoto, I am focused on developing innovative enterprise security solutions. Drawing on over seven years of experience in software development and security, I lead efforts to provide businesses with a cutting-edge confidential e-signature platform. Our platform leverages trusted execution environments in the cloud to ensure robust data protection and privacy for our clients.

My expertise in software infrastructure, passkeys, and web design has been honed through years of working on security-oriented projects, including building confidential computing frameworks and ensuring secure authentication systems. At Subnoto, I remain committed to addressing the critical need for digital security and creating solutions that empower businesses in an increasingly complex cybersecurity landscape.
